BootStrap has its own responsive paddings and margins, and m stands for margins in its classes - mt mb ml mr While p denotes padding - pt pb pl pr While p stands for padding - pt pb pl pr
On the sides, BootStrap Paddings and Margins can be used.
  • t - specifies whether the padding margin should be mt or pt at the top of our element.
  • b - sets either margin of padding could be placed at the bottom of our element: mb or pb.
  • y - sets either margin of padding could be placed at the y-axis of our element on top and bottom: my or py.
  • x - sets either margin of padding could be placed at the x-axis of our element on left and right: my or py.
  • l - sets either margin of padding could be placed at the left most of our element: ml or pl.
  • r - sets either margin of padding could be placed at the right most of our element: mr or pr.
  • blank - sets either margin of padding could be on all sides of our element: m or p.
